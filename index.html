My name is Shirley Wang. I'm a writer from Iowa City. 

In 2018, I wrote a radio story called "“My Dad's Friendship with Charles Barkley"; for NPR’s Only A Game.

It was about my late father and his unlikely friendship with the retired basketball player. You can hear more about this story on the Today Show and in Charles Barkley’s interview with Jimmy Kimmel. I was interviewed by The Washington Post, The Boston Globe, and Iowa Public Radio, among other outlets. It was also mentioned in Sunday Long Read Best of 2018, The New York Times Newsletter and Politico's Playbook, and was listed as NBC's top '13 moments in Asian America that moved us in 2018.' It ran in an issue of Reader's Digest in 2019 and was noted in Best American Sports Writing 2019. 

If you liked this story, you might like reading the follow-up essay I wrote for Father’s Day called How to Grieve in Front Of Millions Of People, or this story about the Chinese Church in Iowa City, where my dad hosted New Year’s parties, or this story I wrote about Vedant Gupta, a 13-year-old sports media influencer, whose dad is also greatly entertaining.
