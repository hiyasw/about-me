My name is Shirley Wang. I'm a writer from Iowa City. 

In 2018, I wrote a radio story called “My Dad's Friendship with Charles Barkley” for NPR’s Only A Game about my late father and his unusual friendship with a retired basketball player. You can hear more about this story from the Today Show and Charles Barkley’s interview with Jimmy Kimmel. I especially loved talking about the story with Charity Nebbe, a talk show host at Iowa Public Radio, who dives deep into me and my dad’s relationship. 

If you liked my story, you might like reading the follow-up I wrote for Father’s Day called How to Grieve in Front Of Millions Of People, or this story about the Chinese Church in Iowa City, where my dad hosted New Year’s parties, or this story I wrote about Vedant Gupta, an 13-year-old sports media influencer, whose dad is also greatly entertaining.
